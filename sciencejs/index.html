Science.js is a p5.js library that makes it easy to build science simulations.

You can draw vectors, make balls move, rotate wheels, etc.

The currently available objects are: Vectors, Free Body Diagrams, Wheels, Kinetic Movers, Moving Backgrounds, and Springs.
